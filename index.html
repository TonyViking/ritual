How to use
Home screen

The home screen stacks top to bottom: your game card, house health, urgent tasks, and category tiles.

⚔️
Game card — XP, rank, and HP bars. Tap the log button to record a negative item.
🏠
House health — reads your chores sheet and shows overdue or due soon count. Tap to open the chores app.
⚠️
Needs attention — your most urgent ritual tasks. Swipe left to mark done, swipe right to quiet until midnight.
📂
Categories — ritual categories as tiles with urgency colours and a 7-day rhythm strip. Tap to see tasks inside.
Ritual tasks
←
Swipe left to mark done — saves to your sheet and awards XP.
→
Swipe right to quiet until midnight — reappears tomorrow as normal.
📂
Tap a category tile to open all tasks in that category, sorted by urgency.

XP and ranking

Completing a ritual task awards +20 XP. Your fantasy rank rises as XP builds and drops if it falls.

🪨
Stone Dweller → 🌿 Wanderer → ⚔️ Squire → 🛡️ Knight → 🌟 Champion → 🔥 Warrior → 💎 Hero → 🦅 Legend → 👑 Sovereign → 🐉 Mythic
HP and health

HP starts at 100. Completing ritual tasks restores HP. It regenerates +5 daily. Below 30 you'll get a care warning.

Negative items reduce HP and XP when logged over their daily limit. They're just tasks in your sheet tagged with the neg category — you add and edit them yourself.

Negative items

Create a category in your sheet: add neg = Negatives 💀 to column I. Then add tasks tagged neg in column E.

📅
Column C (max days) = daily free uses. 0 = always harmful. 4 = free for 4 logs, harmful from the 5th.
❤️
Column B (min days) = HP cost per excess log. Leave 0 to use the default cost.

Swipe left on a negative task to log one occurrence. The card shows today's count and limit. It never disappears from the list — you log as many times as needed.

One-off tasks

Set column F to 1 in your sheet for a task that should only appear once. Marking it done archives it to Sheet2 and removes it from the list.

Sheet layout

A Name · B Min days · C Max days · D Last done · E Category codes · F "1" if one-off · I Legend e.g. self = Self Care 🪥
