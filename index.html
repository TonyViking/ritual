How to use
Your rituals

Rituals are habits organised into categories like Self Care, Mind and Connection. Each has a min and max number of days — the app tracks how long since you last did it.

Home screen

The top strip shows your most urgent rituals across all categories. Below that, category tiles are sorted by urgency — most overdue floats to the top.

←
Swipe left to mark done · Swipe right to hide until tomorrow.
Inside a category

Tap the › pill on a category tile to open it. Use the day filter pills to control how far ahead you see — each category remembers your filter.

🤙
Long press a tile to instantly mark its most overdue ritual done without opening it.
Urgency colours
🟢
Green — recently done, no action needed.
🟡
Yellow/orange — due soon.
🔴
Red — overdue. Time to act.
One-off tasks

Tick One-off when adding for things you only do once. When marked done, they archive to Past One-offs and vanish from the list.

Adding rituals

Tap ＋ to add. Set min days (earliest to repeat) and max days (when it's due). A ritual can belong to multiple categories.
