How to use
Your rituals

Rituals are the habits and routines you track — things you want to do on a loose schedule, like exercise, journalling, skincare, or meditation.

Each ritual has a min days (earliest you'd repeat it) and a max days (when it's due). The app shows how overdue each one is as a colour bar from green to red.

Rituals are grouped by category. Tap a category tile to see its rituals and swipe left to mark one done.

Marking done

Swipe left on a ritual card to mark it done — your sheet is updated immediately. Swipe right to quiet it until midnight 🌙 if you don't want to see it today.

On the home screen, urgent rituals appear in the Needs attention strip. Swipe left there too.

XP and ranks

Marking a ritual done gives you +20 XP. XP builds your rank from Stone Dweller up through Wanderer, Squire, Knight, Champion, Warrior, Hero, Legend, Sovereign, and Mythic. Rank progress shows as a bar on the game card.

HP and health

HP starts at 100. Completing rituals restores small HP. It regenerates +5 daily. Below 30 you'll get a care warning. Negative items reduce HP when over the daily limit. Logging a joy moment gives +2 HP.

Negative items

Negative items live on the home screen under a collapsible heading — tap to expand. Tap any item to log one occurrence. Once over the daily limit it costs HP and XP.

Add them via the ＋ button. Tick Negative item on the form — the fields change to Allowed per day and HP per over allowance. They save to Sheet3.

✨ Joy journal

Tap the ★ star in the header to log a positive moment. The form walks through: what happened · who it was from (you, Kathrina, coworker, family, friend, stranger) · any extra detail on who · the type of moment (love languages or a custom tag) · where you were · how much it meant.

The How much did it mean? slider runs from small flicker to unforgettable. No number is shown — just the position and a phrase.

Tap 📖 Joy journal on the home screen to read back through entries. Newest first. Each entry shows the category, who it was from, location, description, and a row of dots for intensity.

Entries save to Sheet4: Timestamp · WhoFrom · WhoExtra · Category · Location · Description · Intensity.

One-off rituals

Tick One-off when adding a ritual. Once marked done it archives to Sheet2 and disappears from the list. Tap the clock icon (⏱) to view past one-offs.

Categories

Add category codes to column E of your ritual sheet (space-separated). Define each code in column I in the format self = Self Care 🪥. The app caches the legend for 24 hours.

Sheet layout

Sheet1 — rituals. A Name · B Min days · C Max days · D Last done · E Category codes · F One-off flag · I Category legend.

Sheet2 — archived one-off rituals (written by app).

Sheet3 — negative items. A Name · B Allowed/day · C HP/over · D Last logged · E Count today · F Date.

Sheet4 — joy journal. A Timestamp · B WhoFrom · C WhoExtra · D Category · E Location · F Description · G Intensity.

Sheet5 — player progress. Written and read by the app: XP · HP · streak · last check-in. Survives device changes and browser data clears.

Refresh & sign-in

The ⟳ refresh button in the header reloads your data and re-checks your Google sign-in if the session has expired — one tap covers both. Sign-in is silent after the first time.
